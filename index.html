About Green Basket

The Green Basket application, developed by a for-profit business based in Astana, Kazakhstan, serves as a comprehensive tool designed to promote responsible consumption, enhance health and well-being, and improve the grocery shopping experience.

This app features a food scanner functionality, allowing users to scan products using their smartphone's camera and add them to a virtual shopping cart. The cart keeps track of items available at home and those purchased in-store, and it offers recipes based on the ingredients in the virtual basket. Additionally, the app assists users in tracking the shelf life of their groceries, promoting conscious consumption and the rational use of food.

Why We Collect Your Data

Green Basket requests access to your Google account information to provide a seamless shopping experience. We use this data to store your product information securely, manage your virtual cart, and personalize your app experience. Your data helps us suggest recipes, track expiration dates, and maintain a record of your shopping habits to enhance your grocery experience.
