Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03.09.2024

Introduction

Welcome to Green Basket. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our app and related services. By using Green Basket, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

When you use our app, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes:

  • Your Google account information (name, email address) when you sign in using your Google account
  • Details of products you scan and add to your virtual cart
  • User preferences and settings within the app
  • Feedback and support queries

2. Automatically Collected Information

We also collect certain information automatically when you use our app, including:

  • Device information (such as IP address, device ID, and browser type)
  • Usage data (such as pages viewed, time spent on the app, and actions taken)
  • Location data, if enabled on your device, to enhance app functionality and provide location-based services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services, including storing and managing your virtual cart and scanned products
  • To personalize your app experience based on your preferences and usage patterns
  • To send notifications and updates about your shopping cart, product expiration dates, and new features
  • To analyze usage and improve our app functionality and user experience
  • To respond to your support requests and provide customer service
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights, users, and the public

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent or at your direction
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to subpoenas, court orders, or legal processes
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Green Basket, our users, or the public
  • With service providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as data storage and analytics

How We Use Google User Data

When you sign in with your Google account, we access your Google account information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide access to the app
  • Store your preferences and settings for a personalized experience
  • Manage your product data and shopping history associated with your account

We do not use Google user data for any purpose other than those explicitly stated, and we do not share this data with third parties except as described in this policy.

Your Choices and Rights

You have certain rights and choices regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing and updating your personal information through your Google account settings
  • Requesting deletion of your personal data by contacting us
  • Opting out of receiving marketing communications from us
  • Disabling location services to limit the collection of location data

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or within the app. Your continued use of the app after changes have been made will constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.
